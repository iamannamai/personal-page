About Me

Software Engineer, Problem Solver, End User

I've always been an engineer, curious about how and why things work the way they do. That's a large part of how I knew I wanted to be a software engineer. I am fascinated by the way different parts of a system come together to make a product work. Web development has become such an exciting, complex space, with a new challenge at every turn.
